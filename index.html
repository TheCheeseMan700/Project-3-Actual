Well Howdy there user! I am Mr. Helpy, AI assistant to the Razyathian Entity ID Database, and I am here to help you learn the basics of operating this advanced software! First and foremost, you have you there two power buttons, to turn the system on and off as you need, providing you with a blank slate. After that, the rest is easy peasy lemon squeasy! Your territory buttons on the left side select what location of the Razyatian continent you are looking at at the moment. Then, all you need to do is hit that find ID button and see what entities are out there to find! On the right you'll see a few buttons like 'Register New ID' and such. These buttons are for letting you register new entities that need to be added, with the Exit Register Mode button waiting there to take you back to the normal brower. Last but not least, you can save any ID you are lookin at with the Save Current ID button, ain't that neat? Well thats all I have, enjoy your time here. Happy browsing!
